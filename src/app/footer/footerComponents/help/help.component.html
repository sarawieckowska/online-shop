Orders

  • Where is my order?

    Your order will arrive within 3-5 days after it's been placed.You can find a personalised delivery estimate in the shipping confirmation email sent to your email address. So if you're wondering where your order is, please allow up to this date for your order to arrive. A tracking link is provided in your shipping confirmation email and becomes active within 24 hours. You can also track your order in the 'My Orders' section of your account, once the order has been shipped.

  • Can I modify my order after it has been placed?

    Unfortunately, we can’t make changes to your order after it's been finalised. Once an order is placed, the delivery option, delivery address, payment method, and product sizes can't be changed. But you can cancel an order for a limited time after it's been placed; before it’s picked in the warehouse. If this option shows as available to you, we recommend simply cancelling the order and placing a new one.

  • How do I cancel my order?

    If you made a mistake or simply changed your mind after placing an order, there's no need to fuss. As long as your parcel has yet to be picked and packed in our warehouse, you'll have the option to cancel. To cancel your order:

    1. Go to My Orders in your account
    2. Click on view/cancel order*
    3. Click on 'Cancel order' or 'Cancel items' and follow the steps on screen

    Some important points:

    If you redeemed a voucher with your order, the order can only be cancelled in full. Your delivery address can't be changed once you have placed an order, but you can cancel the order and place a new one specifying a different address. Individual Shopper Partner items can't be cancelled. You can only cancel the Partner order in full.

    *If the 'cancel order' option is no longer available, it means your order has been processed in our warehouse and it's unfortunately too late to cancel it.

  • What if an item is out of stock?

    If an item you want is out of stock in your size, you can register to be notified when your size is available again.

    Beside your desired size in the size selection of the item page, just click on 'Notify me'. A pop up will appear where you can enter and submit an email address where the notification will be sent.

    If the size is restocked within the next 3 months, we'll send you an automatic email to let you know. This email does not reserve the item, so if you're still interested then its best to act quickly in case it sells out again.

  • Different orders delivered in one parcel

    In some cases we deliver items from several orders in the same parcel – we will let you know in advance in the delivery confirmation email. You can check whether your parcel contains items from more than one order by logging into your account where you can see which items belong to which order.

    If you need to return items from different orders, you can pack them all in one return parcel.

    We bundle your orders to deliver your items in the quickest, most eco-friendly way possible.

  • How can I place an order?

    To place an order, just follow these steps:

    1. Place an item in your bag: Pick the size you want and select 'Add to bag'. Go to your bag in the top right corner when you have finished shopping.
    2. From inside the bag select 'Go to checkout'.
    3. Select your preferred delivery option. If you have chosen home delivery then confirm your address, if not then select your preferred parcel shop for delivery.
    4. You will be asked to select a payment method. Simply select the payment method you would like, fill in the details where required, and click next.
    5. You are now given the chance to check your order and add voucher codes or Express Delivery. If you are paying by PayPal you will now be redirected to the PayPal website to complete the process.
    6. Once your order has been completed you will see the confirmation on the screen. You will also receive a confirmation email with an order number as a reference and a link to a tracking number once the order is shipped, so that you can track the delivery of your parcel.

  • How can I make sure my delivery address is correct?

    Before completing your order, please make sure that you have entered the correct delivery address. This is displayed on the right side of the screen during the checkout process.

    If you notice you need to change this, just click on 'edit' before completing your order.

  • How do I search for a specific item?

    If you know what you're looking for, type it into the search bar at the top left of Shopper.com and click 'Search' button.

    Search by brand, by product name, or alternatively, search by special occasion, such as ‘wedding’ or ‘party’.

    After this you can also refine your search by category, colour, material, etc. by using the search filters found at the top and at the side of the page.

Returns and Refunds

  • About our return policy

    If you're not happy with your purchase or think your goods arrived damaged, you can send any unworn* item back to us FREE of charge within 100 days of the delivery date.

    *Items worn purely to see if they fit are not considered worn. In the unlikely event that an item is returned to us in an unsuitable condition, we may have to send it back to you. This does not affect your statutory rights.

  • Can I return multiple orders at the same time?

    If you have items from several orders to return to us, simply fill out the return slip for each order, pack each item you are returning along with their return slip in one box, and use a return label from any of the orders (you only need one).

    Keep in mind, this return process might take a little longer as all items have to be booked and delivered back to the correct warehouse(s).

  • What if my item is damaged or defective?

    If an item is damaged or defective, you can file a complaint with us. To process this, we would need:

    • A brief description about how the damage/defect has arisen.
    • 4-5 informative pictures, with a description, for documentation purposes.

    The defect must be clearly visible on at least one of the images. In addition, we would need complete photos of the product e.g. one from the front and one from the back. If the item is a shoe, we also require a picture of the sole.

    You can send the pictures via email to reclamation@Shopper.com. We kindly recommend you to use either ZIP, RAR or JPEG format when sending your pictures. Once we receive the photos, we will review your request as quickly as possible. You will receive feedback from us within a few days on how to proceed.

    We are unable to accept a reclamation for the following defects:

    • Scuffed noses of shoes due to every day usage
    • Damage to the heel of a shoe
    • Damage due to incorrect or intense usage
    • Increased perspiration
    • Colour transfer (i.e. jeans colouring socks blue)
    • Bobbling of the fabric
    • Damage due to external factors, such as friction, sharp objects or chemicals
    • Articles that cause physical discomfort i.e. blisters

  • Paying with Apple Pay

    How can I use Apple Pay?

    Apple Pay makes paying even easier, more secure, and more private than ever before. To start with, make sure you have the latest version of the Shopper app and have registered your card details in the Apple Wallet. You can then make a purchase with a single touch by placing your finger on Touch ID when checking out.

    Apple Pay works with iPhone 6 and later, iPhone SE, iPad Pro, iPad Air 2, and iPad mini 3 and later. You can also use Apple Pay in Safari on any Mac introduced in or after 2012 running macOS Sierra, and confirm the payment with iPhone 6 or later, Apple Watch, or with a MacBook Pro featuring Touch ID.

    It is currently not possible to choose express delivery when paying with Apple Pay. In the event that you would like to send your items to a collection point, please input the address in the payment sheet directly and it will be automatically saved for next time.

    Is Apple Pay Secure?

    Security and privacy are at the core of Apple Pay. When you add a credit or debit card to Apple Pay, the actual card number is neither stored on the device, nor on Apple servers. Instead, a unique number is assigned, encrypted, and securely stored in the Secure Element on your device. Each transaction is authorised with a one-time unique dynamic security code, instead of using the security code from the back of your card.
